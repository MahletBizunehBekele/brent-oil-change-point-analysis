# Assumptions and Limitations

## Assumptions

- Brent crude oil prices reflect major global oil market movements.
- Event dates are approximate and represent the beginning of significant events.
- Market reactions may occur before or after official event dates.
- Daily closing prices adequately represent market behavior.

## Limitations

- Statistical correlation does not imply causation.
- Multiple geopolitical and economic events may overlap.
- Other macroeconomic variables (inflation, exchange rates, GDP, etc.) are not included in this analysis.
- The Bayesian change point model detects structural changes but cannot determine their exact causes.
